Insurance and Billing

We strive to keep costs affordable and ensure complete fee transparency for our patients. We accept most dental insurance plans and will verify your coverage before your appointment.

We accept most dental insurances, including Delta, Regence, United Health, Principal, Humana, etc. When you contact us, we’ll confirm your insurance details and address any questions you may have. For patients without insurance, we also accept cash payments.

Before your appointment, we’ll collect your insurance information and check your coverage. We’ll then provide a cost estimate for your treatment plan. After your visit, we’ll take care of filing and processing your dental claims.

You can book an appointment with us by calling 425-742-0840.
