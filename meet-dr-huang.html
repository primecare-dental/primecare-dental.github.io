Ophelia Huang, DMD

lead dentist

Ophelia Huang grew up in California and earned her Doctor of Dental Medicine degree from Oregon Health & Science University. She is passionate about creating a warm and welcoming environment where every patient feels cared for and heard. Building strong relationships with her patients is one of her top priorities, and she’s dedicated to making every visit as comfortable as possible. In addition to English, she is fluent in both Mandarin and Cantonese.
